# Sidecar Connect Changelog

## [Initial Version] - 2026-05-11

- Manage Sidecar: browse available devices, connect or disconnect, and manage favorites
- Toggle Sidecar: connect or disconnect your preferred device
- Quick Connect Sidecar: connect to your default or favorite device in one step
- Disconnect Sidecar: disconnect all active sessions
- Favorites and recent device history
- Native Swift bridge for fast local Sidecar discovery and connection
